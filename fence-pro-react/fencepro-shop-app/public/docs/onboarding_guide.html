Your Role

Welcome, Production Specialist

Welcome to Superior Fence & Rail, Hudson Valley H.Q. You are now part of a company with over 120 branches, aiming to be the #1 residential fence contractor in the nation.

"You are the backbone of our daily operations. You are the engine that keeps crews moving, yards organized, and jobs starting on time."

Schedule: 5:30 AM to 2:30 PM

Lunch & Breaks: 30 minute unpaid lunch, 20 minute paid break

Overtime: As needed based on job

The Daily Rhythm

High-Level Schedule

Stage + Load — 5:30 AM – 8:00 AM

  • Check chat messages for anything overnight.
  • If jobs: unload, load and strap trailer by 6:30am → Text GM when trailers strapped and ready.
  • Make sure the foreman have job packet and are aware of any key details.
  • Check out any tools.
  • If no jobs, go to next task.

Returns, Organize, Receiving, Admin — 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM

  • Clean and organize yard.
  • Return materials from jobs.
  • Clean and and organize the shop.
  • Receive shipments from previous day and receive truckloads.
  • Note any material, fasteners, or tools needed that is low and notify General Manager.
  • Admin: Complete fence 360 tasks and move BOMs to correct stage in the process.

Stage — 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM

  • Stage for the next jobs. Best to be 2 to 4 days out.
  • Review BOM first with GM for accuracy.
  • Focus on hardest/most time consuming things first: cutting down pickets, gates, transition posts, routing posts.
  • Staging for the following jobs must be complete to 100% by 2pm. (100% doesn't include concrete in the bay. Must have a tote with job pack and labeled.)

Staging Continued & Other Tasks — 12:00 PM – 1:30 PM

  • If staging need, stage jobs.
  • If no staging needed → Build gates, Clean, Review upcoming jobs.

Close + Clean — 1:30 PM – 2:00 PM

  • Checkin with General Manager.
  • Sending Daily Report on daily report channel.
  • Leaving Checklist: Lock gate, Turn off CNC machine, compressor, Forklift inside and doors locked, Trailer cones.

Daily Report on Shop Status

  • 6:30 LOADED (YES / NO): Trailers loaded and strapped by 6:30am. Text GM with update.
  • STAGED TO 100% by 2pm (YES / NO): Jobs staged to 100% + double checked by 2pm day before, best 2 - 3 done.
  • RECEIVING DONE WITHIN 24 HOURS (YES / NO): Materials received and put away. Any receiving errors reported to GM. Paperwork completed properly (initial, date, status).
  • RETURN MATERIAL EVERYDAY (YES / NO): Returned material and hardware put away every day.
  • ORGANIZE + CLEAN (0 to 10): 10 is Yard is free of trash, all material in its home, 0 is the opposite.
  • FENCE360 DONE (YES / NO): Up to date, completed every morning ("Material Checkin", "Stage Job").

Job Purpose & Expectations

Job Purpose

Superior Fence & Rail is an industry leader with 100+ branches across the United States. Our goal is to become the #1 residential fence contractor in the Hudson Valley in the next five years, with customers that love us, and a team excited about growing and the challenges and benefits it provides.

The purpose of the job is to learn and adopt Superior Fence & Rail structured production processes to produce fencing orders in a timely manner while maintaining the integrity of the production ensuring superior workmanship. The typical work week is Monday to Friday from 5:30 am to 2:00 pm, overtime as needed based on the schedule. The job is perfect for folks who love to work hard, excel at being part of a team, wearing multiple hats, doing detailed work, are well organized, flexible, and enjoy the fun of growing and a challenging work environment.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Turn a Bill of Materials into a staged job.
  • Review BOMs for accuracy, report issues to General Manager.
  • Load trailers with material securely and without damaging it; learn and maintain knowledge of how to pull orders.
  • Ensure that all orders are pulled and processed in the appropriate allotted time.
  • Ensure that the yard and work areas are maintained in an organized manner, clean and free of debris at all times.
  • Deliver materials to job sites for subcontractors when needed.
  • Operate the CNC router to fabricate vinyl fence.
  • Perform light carpentry to assemble vinyl and wood gates and other items needs for jobs.
  • Perform inventory counts monthly. Receive material from shipments and check it in properly.
  • Submit daily report on the warehouse status. Perform other duties as assigned.

Superior Candidate

  • Master the Production Specialist position within 3 to 6 months and be promoted to Production Manager with the added responsibilities, pay, and growth opportunities.
  • Consistently have ideas to improve warehouse processes to increase quality, speed, safety, and decrease errors. Work with GM to implement ideas.
  • Become a subject matter expert on fence construction and fence materials.

Background, Experience, Education

  • 2 year of experience working in a warehouse or shop is preferred.
  • 2 years of experience in construction or building is preferred.
  • Forklift experience is preferred.
  • Basic computer skills and proficiency with the internet and G suite products.
  • Must be able to lift 80lbs.
