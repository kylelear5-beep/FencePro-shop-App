Chain Link Fittings Guide

Fittings & Sizing

⚠️ CRITICAL: Distinguish between Outer Diameter (OD) and Inner Diameter (ID) for proper fitting.

Outer Diameter (OD) Fittings

The following fittings depend on the outside measurement of the post:

  • Post Caps (Dome, Acorn)
  • Tension Bands & Brace Bands
  • Post Hinges (Male/Female)

Inner Diameter (ID) Fittings

The following fittings rely on the inside measurement or fit inside the pipe:

  • Rail End Caps
  • Sleeves / Couplers

Gate Frame Sizing

Residential Frames

Typically 1 3/8" to 1 5/8" tubing.

Commercial Frames

Typically 1 5/8" to 1 7/8" tubing.
