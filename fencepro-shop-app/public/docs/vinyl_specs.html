Vinyl Fence Specifications

Vinyl Standards

Material: Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)

High-quality, UV-resistant vinyl. SolarShield™ technology to prevent fading and brittleness.

Wall Thickness

Post thickness: 0.135" to 0.150" depending on style. Rail thickness: 0.080" to 0.120".

Common Styles

Privacy (Tongue & Groove), Semi-Privacy, Picket, and Post-and-Rail.
