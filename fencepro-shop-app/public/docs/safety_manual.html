Safety Training Manual

Shop & Yard Safety

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Eye Protection

Safety glasses must be worn at all times when operating the CNC router, saw, or handling hardware.

Footwear

Steel-toed or composite-toed boots are required in the yard and shop areas.

Hearing Protection

Earplugs or muffs must be worn during high-decibel operations (routing, sawing).

Machine Safety

CNC Operations

Never reach inside the CNC envelope while the machine is powered. Use the E-Stop for any mechanical adjustments.

Forklift Safety

Only certified operators are permitted to drive. Always yield to pedestrians. Lower forks to ground when parked.

Loading & Road Safety

Trailer Weights

Ensure 80-90% of weight is over the axles. Never overload the tongue or the rear.

Strapping

Minimum of two straps per pallet. Check straps after the first 5 miles of transport.
